Translation Invariance and Ergodicity Breaking

Noam Borgnia · TU Munich, 2026

For $\alpha > 0$, the gridlock mechanism ensures that no seeded cluster retains a macroscopic advantage — the dynamics approach a translation-invariant measure. For $\alpha < 0$, the seeded cluster almost surely explodes and the system retains its initial information. We conjecture an ergodicity-breaking phase transition at $\alpha = 0$.
